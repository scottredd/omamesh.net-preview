OmaMesh

Metro Omaha mesh community on Meshtastic & Meshcore

Meshtastic & Meshcore, local and experimental

OmaMesh is a grassroots group of Omaha–Council Bluffs radio nerds exploring long-range, low-power mesh networking. Today we have Meshtastic nodes on rooftops, bikes, and trails — and we’re also starting to experiment with Meshcore in the same region.

Get updates by email Join the conversation on Discord

What is OmaMesh?

We’re a local, volunteer-run community experimenting with off-grid messaging and telemetry. Many of us run Meshtastic nodes today, and some of us are starting to bring up Meshcore-based test nodes as well. Same people, different firmwares and radios.

Two platforms, one community

The local layer is social, not brand-specific. Whether you’re curious about Meshtastic, Meshcore, or just learning what a mesh network is, you’ll find Omaha-area folks comparing notes, sharing coverage results, and testing ideas together.

What this site will offer

Coming soon: getting-started guides, how to join our Meshtastic channels, how to try local Meshcore test networks, coverage and telemetry dashboards, and notes from the experiments we’re running around the metro.
